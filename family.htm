Francoise's on-line family books

Most were made using the Blurb systems, which allows them to be browsed directly on-line by clicking on PREVIEW (no need to buy!). The photobooks are all in Landscape and measure 25cms x 20cms (10"x8").

You can also download the big pdf files we have made from them. They are public on Dropbox, so you can view or download them by invitation.
On a PC with a big enough screen, display them 2 pages at a time to get the effect of the double page spread.
All the family pdfs can also be found on OneDrive .
